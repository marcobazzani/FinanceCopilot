# Plannix - Feature Map

Plannix is an Italian personal finance platform that helps users learn about investing, track their wealth, plan their financial future, and manage investment portfolios. It offers both a free (Smart) and Premium tier.

---

## 1. Dashboard (Il mio Plannix)
- Personalized welcome dashboard
- Learning progress tracker (Processo Plannix completion %)
- Financial advisor card (Il mio Consulente) with rating system
- Financial Health score overview (score out of 100)
- Net worth summary (Patrimonio) with donut chart breakdown
- Plannix Ambassador referral program (earn up to 1,000 EUR credit)
- Notifications center (Notifiche)

---

## 2. IMPARA (Learn)

### 2.1 Processo Plannix
- Structured video course library (51 video lessons)
- Course sections: Benvenuto in Plannix, Il contesto economico-finanziario, Il sistema finanziario, Cosa vogliono gli investitori, Il Processo Plannix, Esplora, Raccogli, Analizza, Sviluppa, Esegui, Revisiona, Market Insights
- Progress tracking per lesson
- Favorites system for lessons
- Instructor profiles (e.g., Luca Lixi - CEO Plannix)

### 2.2 Risorse (Resources Center)
- **Conti Deposito**: Ranking/comparison of deposit accounts
- **Fondi Pensione**: Ranking/comparison of pension funds
- **Gestione Tesoreria** (Business): Treasury management tools
- **Minusvalenze** (Tax Benefit): Capital loss / tax loss harvesting tools
- **Guide e Supporto**: Free guides and support materials
- **Guide Gratuite**: Free downloadable guides on various financial topics
- **Webinar ed eventi**: Webinars and events

---

## 3. TRACCIA (Track)

### 3.1 Patrimonio (Net Worth / Assets)
- Total net worth overview (Totale / Netto toggle)
- Asset categories: Liquidità (Cash), Investimenti (Investments), Immobiliare (Real Estate), Alternativi (Alternatives), Passività (Liabilities)
- Distribution by asset pillar (pie chart)
- Distribution by intermediary/broker (pie chart)
- Add new assets manually

### 3.2 Budgeting
- Income (Entrate) and expense (Spese) tracking
- Savings capacity calculation (Risparmio)
- Essential vs Non-Essential spending breakdown (donut chart)
- Income categories breakdown
- Expense categories breakdown (Categorie / Singole view)
- Switcho integration for expense savings optimization

### 3.3 Investimenti (Investments)
- Investment tracking and monitoring (sub-page under Patrimonio)

### 3.4 Intermediari e Connessioni (Brokers & Connections)
- Open Banking integration via SaltEdge
- Automatic bank account syncing (Fineco, Revolut, etc.)
- Multi-currency support (EUR, CHF, GBP, USD)
- Connection status monitoring and renewal
- Include/exclude accounts from net worth tracking

### 3.5 Salute Finanziaria (Financial Health)
- Overall financial health score (0-100 scale with rating: Scarso, Sufficiente, Buono, Ottimo)
- **Indicatori e Indici (KPIs & Indicators)**:
  - Liquidity indices
  - Real estate indices
  - Debt indices
  - Financial indices
  - Grid and list view modes
- **Analisi Costi (Cost Analysis)**: Investment cost analysis
- Income & Expense summary (Entrate e Spese) with annual figures

### 3.6 Documenti (Documents)
- Document collection and organization system (Raccogli)
- Categories: Liquidità, Immobiliare, Mutui e finanziamenti, Assicurazioni, Investimenti, Altro
- Document status tracking (Fatto / pending)
- Notes and links per document
- Completion percentage per category

---

## 4. PIANIFICA (Plan)

### 4.1 Piano Finanziario (Financial Plan)
- Create and manage financial plans
- Active plan tracking with objectives and monthly targets
- Draft plans support
- Multiple plan scenarios

### 4.2 Esplora (Explore)
- Goal-discovery exercises:
  - "Le 3 domande di George Kinder" (George Kinder's 3 Questions)
  - "L'Esercizio dei perché" (The Why Exercise)
- Tools to identify investment priorities and life goals

### 4.3 Simulatori (Simulators/Calculators)
- **Simulatore del capitale futuro**: Future capital simulator (compound interest)
- **Calcolatore rendita da capitale**: Capital income calculator (how much capital needed to live off)
- **Simulatore creazione portafoglio modello**: Model portfolio creation simulator
- **Simulatore pensionistico**: Pension simulator (retirement, disability, survivors)
- **Coming soon**:
  - Costo dell'università per i figli (University cost for children)
  - Comparatore affitto vs mutuo (Rent vs mortgage comparator)
  - Capitale umano da assicurare (Human capital to insure)
  - Calcolatore inflazione e potere d'acquisto (Inflation & purchasing power calculator)
  - Calcolatore della rendita immobiliare (Real estate income calculator)

---

## 5. INVESTI (Invest)

### 5.1 Portafogli (Portfolios)
- Multiple portfolio management with tracking
- Portfolio details: instruments count, date, asset class breakdown
- Asset class visualization (Monetario, Azionario, etc.)
- Unassigned investments view
- Create new portfolios
- **Piani di Accumulo (DCA Plans)**: Dollar-cost averaging / accumulation plans
- **Ribilanciamento (Rebalancing)**: Portfolio rebalancing tools
- **Entrata a mercato (Market Entry)**: Market entry strategy tools
- **Modelli (Models)**: Model/template portfolios
- **Elenco eventi (Events List)**: Portfolio events log

### 5.2 Rendix (Premium Add-on)
- Separate premium module (requires Rendix package subscription)
- Advanced investment features (access restricted)

---

## 6. Other Features

### 6.1 Plannix Ambassador Program
- Referral program to earn up to 1,000 EUR credit
- Share Plannix with others

### 6.2 Supporto (Support)
- In-app support access
- Live chat widget (bottom-right corner)

### 6.3 User Profile
- Account management
- Subscription tier display (Smart / Premium)
- Upgrade to Premium option

### 6.4 Financial Advisor Integration
- Assigned consultant (Consulente) profile
- Advisor rating system
- Direct communication channel

---

## Subscription Tiers
- **Plannix Smart** (Free): Core features with limited access
- **Plannix Premium**: Full access to all features
- **Rendix Add-on**: Advanced investment module (separate package)
